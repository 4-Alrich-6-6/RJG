Data Privacy and Personal Information

Since your platform uses personal information for resumes, you must comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

  • Purpose of Collection: Explicitly state that personal data (name, contact details, work history, etc.) is collected solely for job matching and recruitment.
  • User Consent: Require users to provide informed consent before their resume data is shared with potential employers.
  • Data Accuracy (Rectification): Users have the right to access and correct any inaccurate personal information in their profiles at any time.
  • Data Retention: Clearly define how long you will store resumes (e.g., 12 months) before they are archived or deleted.

Truthfulness of Information

To maintain the platform's integrity, include a clause on the "Duty of Truthfulness" for both job seekers and employers.

  • For Candidates: State that misrepresenting qualifications, experience, or identity is strictly prohibited and can lead to account termination.
  • For Employers: Mandate that all job postings must be authentic, accurate, and non-discriminatory.
  • Verification: Include a disclaimer that while you provide the platform, the final responsibility for verifying information lies with the parties involved in the hiring process.

Age Requirements & Parental Consent

To ensure safety and compliance with data privacy standards, we enforce strict age-based controls. Legal protections for minors require us to verify eligibility and ensure proper guardian guidance.

  • Minimum Age: You must be at least 15 years old to create an account on our platform.

  • Liability: Users who ignore these requirements do so at their own risk; the developer is not liable for such actions.

Netiquette and Professional Conduct

Establish rules for how users should interact to ensure a respectful digital environment.

  • Professional Language: Discourage the use of slang, coarse language, or all-caps (which signifies shouting) in communications.
  • Responsiveness: Encourage punctual responses to job inquiries and messages to show respect for others' time.
  • Respect Privacy: Forbid users from sharing private communications or screenshots without the other party's explicit permission.
